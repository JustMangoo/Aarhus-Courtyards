About Aarhus Courtyards

Aarhus Courtyards is a passion project born from a love of urban exploration and a desire to share the joy of discovering hidden gems in the city. We believe that everyone deserves access to peaceful escapes and the beauty of nature, even in the heart of the city. Explore, connect, and find your own slice of tranquility with Aarhus Courtyards.

FAQ

What is Aarhus Courtyards?
Aarhus Courtyards is a platform designed to help you discover hidden courtyards in the city of Aarhus. Explore and enjoy these peaceful urban oases!
How are the courtyards selected?
Courtyards are selected based on user submissions and our own research. We prioritize spaces that offer tranquility, natural beauty, and a unique urban experience.
How do I find courtyards near me?
Use the interactive map or search function to locate courtyards in your desired area. You can also filter results by features such as greenery, seating, and accessibility.
Will my personal details be public?
No, your personal information will be kept private and used only for platform functionality and communication purposes.
Who do I contact to report errors?
You can report errors or submit feedback by contacting us through the "Contact Us" page or by emailing us at updates@aarhuscourtyards.com
