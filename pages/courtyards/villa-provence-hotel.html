Villa Provence Hotel

Step through the arched doorway beside Villa Provence Hotel and discover a hidden oasis – the charming courtyard. Transformed from a simple patio, this tranquil space offers a delightful escape from the city bustle. Lush greenery, including fragrant lavender bushes and climbing vines, creates a serene atmosphere, while comfortable seating areas invite guests to relax and unwind. In the evenings, warm fairy lights twinkle overhead, casting a magical glow.

The courtyard is a delightful spot to enjoy a cup of coffee or a glass of wine from the hotel's cafe. On select evenings, the space comes alive with the gentle melodies of live acoustic performances – the perfect accompaniment to a relaxing evening under the Provençal sky.
