Café Mellemfolk

Tucked away behind the cozy Café Mellemfolk, this courtyard has been a quiet retreat in Aarhus since 2015. Originally used as a storage area for the café’s supplies, the space was transformed into a welcoming green oasis by a group of volunteers. Inspired by the café’s focus on community and sustainability, they planted lush greenery, added rustic seating, and strung up warm fairy lights to create an intimate atmosphere.

Today, the courtyard is a hidden gem where visitors can sip coffee, enjoy the occasional live acoustic performance, or simply unwind in the tranquil embrace of nature.
