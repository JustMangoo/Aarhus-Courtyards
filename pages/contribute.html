Rules to keep in mind

1. Keep it Friendly: Share only public or accessible courtyards that everyone can enjoy.
2. Be Descriptive: Provide as much detail as you can about the courtyard to help others discover its charm.
3. Focus on Positivity: Highlight what makes the space relaxing, unique, or special.
4. Respect Privacy: Avoid sharing personal or private information, like residential courtyards not meant for public access.
5. No Spam or Ads: Keep submissions relevant to the project — no advertisements or unrelated content.
6. Be Honest: Only submit places you’ve visited and genuinely think are worth sharing.

These rules ensure the form stays user-friendly and the submissions are valuable for the community.
